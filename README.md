# Side Hustle Revenue Calculator

A free, instant calculator to estimate your monthly income from selling digital goods online — social media boosts, gift cards, crypto vouchers, premium accounts, and more.

## Features

- Real-time revenue calculation
- 6-month growth forecast
- Platform fee and expense modeling
- Shareable links (encode your numbers in URL)
- Zero dependencies, runs fully offline
